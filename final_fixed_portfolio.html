03 — Process
Step 01
Brief
We start with a conversation. Understanding your goals, audience, tone, and deliverables so every edit is intentional from the start.
Step 02
Asset Collection
You share footage, brand kit, references, and music preferences. The cleaner the input, the sharper the output.
Step 03
Rough Cut
I assemble the structure — pacing, flow, and sequence. This is where ideas become something you can actually see.
Step 04
Motion & Sound
Graphics, animations, colour grade, SFX, and music layering — the polish that transforms a cut into an experience.
Step 05
Review
You watch, react, and give feedback. I refine until it's exactly what you envisioned — or better than you expected.
Step 06
Delivery
Final export in your required format and resolution. Platform-optimised, deadline-ready, and ready to go live.
